WHEN WAS THE GUNSHOP FOUNDED

The gunshop was established in 1879. Founder being Mr.Gunnerton. The gun shop original location was in Europe, Romainia, before branching out to the USA and England. We can equipt you with guns like Ruger GP100, Sig Sauer P226 MK-25, Glock 41 Gen4, Colt Close Quarter Battle Pistol (CQBP), Beretta M9A1, Glock 19, S&W Governor, Ruger SP101 ETC.
